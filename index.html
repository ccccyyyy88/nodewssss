Economy / 经济

常见主张 / Common themes

  • 减税以刺激企业与个人投资。 / Tax reduction to stimulate business and personal investment.
  • 优先以国内产业与就业为重点的贸易政策。 / Trade policies prioritizing domestic industry and jobs.
  • 监管调整以降低企业合规成本。 / Regulatory reform to lower compliance costs for businesses.

Immigration / 移民

常见主张 / Common themes

  • 加强边境安全和移民执法。 / Strengthen border security and immigration enforcement.
  • 优先基于技能或就业资质的移民筛选。 / Prioritize immigration selection based on skills or employment qualifications.
  • 改革签证与庇护程序以提高效率或减少滥用。 / Reform visa and asylum procedures to improve efficiency or reduce misuse.
注：移民议题牵涉法律、人权与经济多个层面，政策效果常因实施细节而异。 / Note: immigration involves law, human rights, and economics; outcomes depend heavily on implementation details.

Foreign Policy / 对外政策

常见主张 / Common themes

  • 优先本国利益并重新谈判被认为对本国不利的协议。 / Prioritize national interests and renegotiate agreements seen as unfavorable.
  • 在贸易与安全上采取更强硬的立场以获得谈判筹码。 / Adopt tougher stances in trade and security to gain negotiating leverage.
  • 强调盟友责任分担与自力更生。 / Emphasize burden-sharing by allies and self-reliance.

中立说明 / Neutral note

对外政策的短期冲击与长期战略效果常不同，学术与智库存在多样观点。 / Short-term impacts and long-term strategic effects can differ; scholars and think tanks hold diverse views.

Energy & Environment / 能源与环境

常见主张 / Common themes

  • 支持化石燃料开发以保证能源独立与就业。 / Support fossil fuel development to ensure energy independence and jobs.
  • 在环境监管与能源生产间寻求平衡。 / Seek balance between environmental regulation and energy production.
  • 鼓励国内能源生产与基础设施投资。 / Encourage domestic energy production and infrastructure investment.

说明：能源与环境政策会影响经济、气候与公共健康，评估常依赖跨学科数据。 / Note: energy and environment policies affect economy, climate, and public health; assessment relies on interdisciplinary data.
